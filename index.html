About Me

I'm a 22-year-old graduate from Florida State University with a bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems and a minor in Computer Science. Born and raised in Tallahassee, FL, I'm passionate about technology, problem-solving, and hands-on projects—and I'm always eager to learn something new. I love riding my bikes, building things, taking things apart, and getting my hands dirty.

Currently, I'm working on my biggest project yet: Jamble—an app designed to help you plan and host custom game night events with friends. Whether it’s a board game, video game, or anything in between, I want to make organizing a game night simple and fun.

Feel free to reach out via email or any of the platforms below!
